### Areas Outside CareerIQ's Scope

The following W!SE/Jump$tart topic areas are **not covered** by CareerIQ and should be addressed through direct classroom instruction and W!SE curriculum resources:

- Banking products (checking, savings, CDs, money market accounts)
- Credit cards, APR, minimum payments
- Credit scores and credit reports
- Insurance (health, auto, renter's)
- Taxes (income tax, W-4, filing)
- Investment basics (stocks, bonds, mutual funds, 401k)
- Fraud, identity theft, and consumer protection

CareerIQ is designed as a **focused supplement** for the income, debt, and budgeting units — not a comprehensive curriculum replacement.

## Jump$tart National Standards Alignment

CareerIQ addresses competencies within the following Jump$tart standard domains:

**Standard 1 — Employment and Income**
- 9–12 Competency 1.1: Analyze how career choice, education, skills, and economic conditions affect income
- 9–12 Competency 1.2: Explain how taxes and other deductions affect take-home pay

**Standard 4 — Borrowing**
- 9–12 Competency 4.1: Evaluate the costs and benefits of borrowing money
- 9–12 Competency 4.3: Summarize the terms of a loan agreement
- 9–12 Competency 4.5: Calculate the cost of borrowing money

**Standard 2 — Spending and Saving**
- 9–12 Competency 2.1: Develop a personal budget that includes planning for spending, saving, and giving
